Welcome to FETP Bangladesh – Intermediate

The Intermediate Field Epidemiology Training Program (FETP) strengthens public health surveillance, outbreak investigation, and emergency response capacity in Bangladesh.

The program operates under the Institute of Epidemiology, Disease Control and Research (IEDCR), Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Bangladesh.
